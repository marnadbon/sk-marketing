## Implementation Notes

### Sending Schedule
- **Day 1**: Email 1 (Doors open soon)
- **Day 2**: Email 2 (Case Study #1)
- **Day 3**: Email 3 (Case Study #2)
- **Day 4**: Email 4 (Case Study #3 - deep framework)
- **Day 5**: Email 5 (Speed wins)
- **Day 6**: Email 6 (Open the doors - full offer)
- **Day 7**: Email 7 (Questions follow-up)
- **Day 8**: Email 8 (Proof)
- **Day 9**: Email 9 (Close the doors)

### Key Principles
- **Send daily.** Don't skip days or space them out.
- **Every email has a CTA.** Either "SKIP" (early access), "CASE STUDY" (early bird), or "IN" (buy).
- **Reply-based selling.** When someone replies, send them the offer details personally and close in DM/email.
- **Real scarcity.** Pick a real number of spots. Cross them out publicly as people join.
- **Vary case studies on rerun.** When you run this again in 60 days, use different case studies.
- **Vary bonuses on rerun.** Change one bonus per cycle to keep it fresh.
- **Don't discount, bonus instead.** Weekly pricing eliminates the need for discounts.

### If You Don't Have Case Studies Yet
Remove emails 3 and 4 (the two extra case studies). Keep email 2 with your best single case study or replace it with your own Epiphany Bridge story - how YOU discovered Vibecoding and what it changed for your business.

Abbreviated sequence without case studies:
- Day 1: Email 1 (Doors open soon)
- Day 2: Email 2 (Your story OR one case study)
- Day 3: Email 5 (Speed wins)
- Day 4: Email 6 (Open the doors)
- Day 5: Email 7 (Questions)
- Day 6: Email 8 (Proof)
- Day 7: Email 9 (Close the doors)

### Pricing Engineering
- **Lead with weekly**: "$100/week" not "$500"
- **Pay-in-full in cart only**: Don't mention it in emails. Let the checkout page show "Save $100 - pay $400 today"
- **30-35% discount for pay-in-full** triggers 50-80% adoption

### After the Campaign
- People who replied but didn't buy → add to waitlist for next cycle
- People who bought → start post-purchase sequence selling the Vibe Vault membership
- Run again in 60 days with different case studies and one changed bonus

### Adapting for Other Offers
This same sequence works for:
- The Vibe Automation Workshop ($97) - shorter commitment, higher conversion
- The Vibe Automation Intensive ($200/week) - when you're ready to launch it
- Any future offer - just swap the promise, case studies, and pricing
